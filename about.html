CONCEPT

The life of an animal is worth more than human fashion desires.

HUTLER aims to produce beautiful, empowering bags for discerning, style-led females.
With a strong focus on animal welfare, the collection will be of minimalistic and functional design created using high-quality vegan leather.

The brand name, HUTLER, deranges from the combination of Hannah and Butler, the founder’s name.
This gives the brand a sense of personal identity, meaning that every time something is purchased from the brand, a piece of the founder’s heart and creativity is within it.
